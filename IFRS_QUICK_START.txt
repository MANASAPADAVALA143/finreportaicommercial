========================================
HOW TO ACCESS:
========================================

1. Open browser: http://localhost:3000/dashboard

2. Click "IFRS Statement Generator" card (orange icon)

3. Upload your Trial Balance Excel/CSV file
   OR
   Click "Try with Sample Data"

4. Review AI mappings (auto-suggests with confidence scores)

5. Click "Generate IFRS Statements"

6. Export to your preferred format!

========================================
REQUIRED FILE FORMAT:
========================================

Your Trial Balance needs these columns:
- Account Code
- Account Name
- Account Type (Asset, Liability, Equity, Revenue, Expense)
- Debit
- Credit

Example:
Account Code | Account Name         | Account Type | Debit   | Credit
1000         | Cash in Bank         | Asset        | 500000  | 0
4000         | Sales Revenue        | Revenue      | 0       | 3200000
5000         | Cost of Goods Sold   | Expense      | 1450000 | 0

========================================
WHAT YOU GET:
========================================

1. Statement of Financial Position (Balance Sheet)
2. Statement of Profit or Loss (Income Statement)  
3. Statement of Cash Flows (Indirect Method)
4. Statement of Changes in Equity

All formatted according to IFRS standards!
