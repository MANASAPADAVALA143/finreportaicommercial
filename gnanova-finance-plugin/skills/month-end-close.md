## Month End Close Skill

Guide finance teams through month-end close checklist:

1. Journal entry validation
2. Account reconciliation
3. IFRS/Ind AS compliance check
4. Anomaly detection on transactions
5. Final P&L commentary generation

Call FinReportAI anomaly detection (`/excel/analyze?analysis_type=anomaly`) for journal-style or trial-balance extracts. Flag SOD violations immediately as HIGH priority.
